About

I'm Muskan, originally from Patna, Bihar. I completed my Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) at IIT Patna, where my interest in business strategy, marketing, and organisational growth really took shape.

"I'd rather understand people first — strategy works best once you know who it's for."

I'm a sociable, hardworking, and kind-hearted person who genuinely enjoys helping others and building relationships that last. Outside of academics, I read novels and play badminton — both have quietly taught me discipline, focus, and how to work well as part of a team.

Through my coursework and internship experience, I've developed a real interest in understanding how businesses grow, how people make decisions, and how the two connect. I'm always looking for the next thing to learn.
